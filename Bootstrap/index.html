About Me

I visited Puerto Rico before I started the bootcamp. That’s why I want to include pictures from my trip in this portfolio. Here is some information that may persuade you to go visit the beautiful islands. Puerto Rico has stunning and beautiful there are beaches for surfing, with tranquil water, but all of them are perfect for relaxation and enjoying a Pina colada. I visited the old San Juan at least 5 times, you can’t get enough of those colorful buildings. I love their food especially their local dish mofongo, you can see that they cook with passion.
